Feature 03

QR Code Location Finder

Physical QR signs at every trail junction, lift, and viewpoint. Guests scan → instantly know where they are, what's nearby, and which trail to take next. Works with zero GPS and zero internet.

✓ Solves "I'm lost" without draining battery with GPS
✓ 100% reliable in dead-signal mountain zones
✓ Shows nearby trails, POIs, and recommended routes
